REQ-010 — Score 90 — À risque

Texte exigence :

The onboard unit shall exchange messages with wayside within 200 ms in all conditions.

Issues détectées :

  • MINOR — Unbounded scope detected ('all/any conditions'): define operating conditions and assumptions.

Suggestions IA : IA

  • Rewrite requirement: The onboard unit shall achieve application-layer round-trip time (RTT) ≤ 200 ms for ≥ 99% of 10,000 message exchanges of 1,024-byte payload with the designated wayside endpoint under Operating Conditions OC-010.
  • Define Operating Conditions OC-010: train speed 0–160 km/h; ambient temperature −25 to +55 °C; RF RSSI at onboard antenna ≥ −85 dBm; MAC-layer packet loss ≤ 1%; message payload 1,024 bytes; IPsec enabled.
  • Acceptance criteria: In OC-010 scenarios (stationary, 80 km/h, 160 km/h × temperatures −25, 25, 55 °C × RSSI targets −70, −85 dBm), measured RTT median ≤ 120 ms and 99th percentile ≤ 200 ms over 10,000 exchanges per scenario; timeouts (>1 s) ≤ 1%.
