REQ-001 — Score 100 — OK

Texte exigence :

The braking system shall stop the train within 800 m when the emergency brake is applied at 160 km/h on dry track.

Issues détectées :

Aucune issue détectée.

Suggestions IA : AI

  • Requirement: At maximum operating mass on straight, level, dry track (adhesion µ ≥ 0.25), when an emergency brake command is issued at 160 km/h ±1 km/h, the train shall reach 0 km/h within 800 m measured from command (t0) to stop.
  • Acceptance criteria: 20 runs under stated conditions; in each run stopping distance ≤ 800 m; initial speed 160 ±1 km/h; distance measured by calibrated odometry (±2 m) from brake command to v=0.
  • Acceptance criteria (environment): Track gradient ±0.1%, straight track (radius ≥ 5 km), wind ≤ 5 m/s, no precipitation for ≥ 2 h, railhead dry verified with adhesion test µ ≥ 0.25.
REQ-002 — Score 70 — À risque

Texte exigence :

The HMI should display the status quickly and clearly for the driver.

Issues détectées :

  • [MINOR] Modal verb 'should' detected (weak commitment). Prefer 'shall' or measurable phrasing.
  • [MINOR] Ambiguous term 'quick' detected (not measurable).
  • [MINOR] Acceptance criteria missing (verification might be unclear).

Suggestions IA : AI

  • Remplacer les modaux faibles (should/may/…) par une formulation normative mesurable (shall + métriques).
  • Remplacer les termes qualitatifs par des critères quantifiés (temps, taux, seuils, tolérances).
  • Ajouter des critères d’acceptation concrets (Given/When/Then, seuils, tolérances).
  • Rewrite requirement: 'The HMI shall display the current train status within 1.0 s of receiving a status change event.'
  • Add requirement: 'The train status indicator shall be persistently visible on all HMI screens, in the top-right quadrant, with text height ≥ 6 mm and contrast ratio ≥ 7:1.'
  • Add acceptance criteria (Inspection): AC1: Status indicator present on all screens; AC2: Update latency ≤ 1.0 s (observed with stopwatch/video); AC3: Text height ≥ 6 mm; AC4: Contrast ratio ≥ 7:1; AC5: Status shown by both text and icon.
REQ-003 — Score 100 — OK

Texte exigence :

The infusion pump shall deliver 5 mL/h ± 5% for flow rates between 1 mL/h and 10 mL/h at 23°C with saline.

Issues détectées :

Aucune issue détectée.

Suggestions IA : AI

  • Revise requirement: For setpoints {1, 3, 5, 7, 10} mL/h at 23°C ±2°C with 0.9% saline, the average delivered volume over 60 min shall be within ±5% of the setpoint.
  • Acceptance criteria: Measure gravimetrically (balance resolution ≤0.01 g, uncertainty ≤0.5%, NIST-traceable); convert using saline density 1.004 g/mL at 23°C; exclude first 5 min stabilization; sample ≥1 Hz.
  • Acceptance criteria: Verify at downstream backpressure 100 ±10 mmHg using a 180 cm PVC infusion set; bag height adjusted for 0 cm head relative to pump outlet; ambient 23°C ±2°C, RH 30–70%.
