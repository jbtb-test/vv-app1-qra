REQ-001 — Score 100 — OK

Texte exigence :

The braking system shall stop the train within 800 m when the emergency brake is applied at 160 km/h on dry track.

Issues détectées :

Aucune issue détectée.

Suggestions IA : IA

  • Requirement: Stop a train at maximum operating mass from 160.0 km/h (±0.5 km/h) to 0 km/h within 800 m on straight, level track (|gradient| ≤ 1‰, curve radius ≥ 3000 m) with wheel–rail adhesion μ ≥ 0.25.
  • Acceptance: Start distance at emergency brake command; stopping condition = speed ≤ 0.5 km/h sustained for ≥2 s; distance measured with calibrated odometry/GNSS (total error ≤ ±2 m); each of 10 valid runs ≤ 800 m.
  • Acceptance setup: Ambient 5–35°C, wind ≤ 5 m/s, dry rail verified by adhesion μ ≥ 0.25 pre-test, brake system nominal (no faults), train mass = maximum operating mass ±1%.
REQ-002 — Score 70 — À risque

Texte exigence :

The HMI should display the status quickly and clearly for the driver.

Issues détectées :

  • MINOR — Modal verb 'should' detected (weak commitment). Prefer 'shall' or measurable phrasing.
  • MINOR — Ambiguous term 'quick' detected (not measurable).
  • MINOR — Acceptance criteria missing (verification might be unclear).

Suggestions IA : IA

  • Remplacer les modaux faibles (should/may/…) par une formulation normative mesurable (shall + métriques).
  • Remplacer les termes qualitatifs par des critères quantifiés (temps, taux, seuils, tolérances).
  • Ajouter des critères d’acceptation concrets (Given/When/Then, seuils, tolérances).
  • The HMI shall update the displayed train status within 1.0 s after receiving a status change from the control system.
  • The HMI shall present status information with text x-height ≥ 6 mm, icons ≥ 10 mm, and foreground/background luminance contrast ratio ≥ 7:1 at the installed display.
  • Acceptance criteria: For 100 status changes, ≥95% of updates appear within 0.5 s and none exceed 1.0 s; measured text x-height ≥ 6 mm; icon size ≥ 10 mm; contrast ratio ≥ 7:1; each status includes a non-color cue (text or icon).
REQ-003 — Score 100 — OK

Texte exigence :

The infusion pump shall deliver 5 mL/h ± 5% for flow rates between 1 mL/h and 10 mL/h at 23°C with saline.

Issues détectées :

Aucune issue détectée.

Suggestions IA : IA

  • Reword requirement: For any setpoint S in 1–10 mL/h, delivered flow averaged over 60 min shall be within ±5% of S at 23°C ±2°C using 0.9% saline and outlet backpressure 100 mmHg ±10 mmHg.
  • Acceptance criteria: Test S ∈ {1, 5, 10} mL/h; after ≤10 min stabilization, the 60‑min average is within ±5% of S and each contiguous 10‑min interval average is within ±10%.
  • Acceptance criteria: Measure gravimetrically using a calibrated balance (resolution ≤0.01 g, uncertainty ≤0.02 g) with sealed collector; convert mass to volume using saline density 1.003 g/mL at 23°C ±1°C.
REQ-004 — Score 90 — À risque

Texte exigence :

The ECU shall provide diagnostic information if needed.

Issues détectées :

  • MINOR — Ambiguous term 'if needed' detected (not measurable).

Suggestions IA : IA

  • Remplacer les termes qualitatifs par des critères quantifiés (temps, taux, seuils, tolérances).
  • Rewrite requirement: The ECU shall provide diagnostic information upon receiving a diagnostic read request from an external tester on the vehicle network.
  • Add acceptance criterion: The ECU shall transmit the diagnostic information within 500 ms of request receipt.
  • Rewrite requirement: Upon setting a new DTC, the ECU shall store the diagnostic information (DTC code, timestamp, operating mode) and make it available for subsequent diagnostic reads.
